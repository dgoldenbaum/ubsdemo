Sustainable investing UBS Manage Advanced [Sustainable Investing]

Sustainable investing gives you the chance to influence society and the environment in positive ways. With UBS Manage Advanced [Sustainable Investing] you can invest to achieve financial returns and make a measurable social and ecological impact.

Why invest sustainably?

Did you know that sustainable investing is one of the fastest growing areas in finance? Around a third of Swiss investors are already investing sustainably and this trend remains strong.

And the best thing: more than 2,000 studies have shown that sustainable investments offer comparable or indeed better potential returns than traditional portfolios.

Our sustainable investment strategies are primarily based on the United Nations' 17 sustainable development goals in line with ESG criteria.
